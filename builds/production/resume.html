Employment

Smartforms and JavaScript Developer contract

I was brought on board to CASA to continue with the creation and updating of web accessible PDFs and smart forms using the Adobe javascript API. I began by troubleshooting and fixing existing bugs in the forms and continued with creating new forms and updating older ones. My role later expanded into the creation of workflows and processes for the customer service team to use with the new software being implemented as part of the larger project I was part of. I also became part of the team providing training on how to use the software.

CrimTrac 2012 - 2014

Web and UI Designer full-time

My primary role was to design User Interfaces for Agency stakeholders. In this role I met with stakeholders and other project staff to determine the best workflow for users and primary goals of the web application. I worked on three applications targeted at various policing audiences and one for public use. During the design of these applications I employed best web standards and practices in determining layout, flow, the display of content and web accessibility of the interfaces. I also used research, reports and in-house usability testing to support my decisions or to further develop the interfaces.
